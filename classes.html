Our Classes

Enjoy small, guided group classes in our CBD studios: Telok Ayer and Robinson Road, and our Outdoor Bootcamp at Fort Canning.

Purchase your class packs here.

View Class Schedule



SWEAT

Movement is a gift, and our Sweat classes allow you to get straight in on the action.

Sweat is High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) at its finest, and as the name suggests, bring a change of clothes.

Sweat classes are circuit style classes involving light weights and bodyweight movements in a HIIT format.

Sweat classes are 45 minutes long, and is open to all levels.

STRUCTURE

If you’ve never stepped into a gym, this is a technique-focused class that will help you understand the language of movement: Push, Pull, Hinge, Squat.

Structure classes are fundamental movement classes where we teach you how to move with classic gym equipment like barbells, dumbbells and kettlebells..

Structure classes are 45 minutes long, and is open to all levels.

SKILL

Skill is our flagship class and is led by coaches who are or have been competitive athletes.

Skill classes are aimed at bringing your physical skills in weightlifting, gymnastics and metabolic conditioning to a level you’ve never dreamed of before. Skill classes are more demanding, with movements like rope climbs, barbell cycling under fatigue and Interval Weight Training.

Skill classes are 60 minutes long. You must be able to do a STRUCTURE class comfortably before booking this class.

SPARTAN SKILL

Our Spartan Skill class is where we go deep into the mechanics of the Skill portion of the Spartan race.

We’ll break down the levers behind crawling, rope climbs, monkey bars, grip and carries in a way that takes the guesswork out of your problem areas, so you can focus on what matters: the race.

This class runs for 60 minutes, and is led by George.

STRUCTURE+

Structure+ is an addition to our repertoire of Structure classes, with an added focus on coordination and weight transfer skills.

Structure+ builds on the knowledge of the hinge, squat, push and pull, elevating those techniques with concepts of explosiveness, agility and coordination.

This class runs for 60 minutes, and is led by Coach Wu ChuanFu, former coach of the Singapore Weightlifting Team.

BARBELL CLUB

Our Barbell club focuses on increasing technical proficiency in the snatch and the clean and jerk within a small group setting.

Because of the progressive and personal nature of your experience with these lifts, we offer admission to the program by monthly membership only.

The Barbell club is led by Coach Wu ChuanFu, former coach of the Singapore Weightlifting Team.
